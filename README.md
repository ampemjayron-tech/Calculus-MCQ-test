To share a permanent link that anyone can open without downloading anything:

1. Go to your repository: **[https://github.com/ampemjayron-tech/Calculus-MCQ-test](https://github.com/ampemjayron-tech/Calculus-MCQ-test)**

3. Scroll down to **"Pages"** in the left sidebar
4. Under **"Branch"**, select `main` and folder `/root (/)`, then click **Save**
5. After ~2 minutes, your test will be live at:

   ```
   https://ampemjayron-tech.github.io/Calculus-MCQ-test/calculus-mcq-test.html
   ```

   Share that link with anyone — they can open it directly in their browser!
